Overview: When VisKo Cannot Answer a Visualization Query

VisKo may not be able to visualize your dataset because of a number of reasons including:
  • It cannot process your data in the format it currently resides
  • It does not know how generate the requested view
  • It does not know how to transform your data into another required intermediate format
  • One or more parameters were not bound to a value

If you understand the fitness of your knowledge base, you may be able understand what knowledge you need to add for VisKo to be able to process your visualization queries.

This page will describe how you can ascertain the fitness of your VisKo knowledge base instance through querying and visual analysis techniques described below.
